Privacy Policy

The AI Testing Company (“Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit or use our website, web application, and associated services (“Platform”).

By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Platform.

---

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Account information (e.g., name, email address, password)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)
  • Usage data (e.g., quizzes taken, time spent on platform)
  • Device & browser data (e.g., IP address, operating system)
  • Generated content data (e.g., your questions, answers, study history)

Some information may be generated or processed by AI models to personalize your experience.

---

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Deliver and personalize learning experiences
  • Generate voice lectures, diagrams, and explanations
  • Provide 1:1 tutoring via AI avatars
  • Improve our models and product features
  • Communicate with you about updates or new features
  • Process payments for subscriptions or usage

We may also use anonymized and aggregated data for analytics, product development, and research.

---

3. Technologies and Providers We Use

Our product is built using AI technologies and services provided by external vendors. These include:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs): e.g., OpenAI GPT-4o, Claude, DeepSeek
  • Text-to-Speech: ElevenLabs for voice lectures
  • Image Generation: OpenAI API for custom diagrams
  • Realtime Speech API: For speech-driven interfaces
  • Video Generation: OmniHuman-1 (or similar SOTA models) for avatar lectures
  • Payment Processing: Stripe
  • Web Analytics and Hosting: Common JavaScript-based tools and services

Each of these services may collect limited data for operational purposes. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details:

  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy
  • ElevenLabs: https://www.elevenlabs.io/privacy
  • Stripe: https://stripe.com/privacy
  • [Insert others as needed]

Note: We actively monitor and may substitute technologies in our stack as the ecosystem evolves rapidly. Any change in third-party processors will be reflected in the “Last Updated” date of this Privacy Policy.

---

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your data:

  • With service providers who help us run the Platform (e.g., cloud hosting, payments, AI APIs)
  • When legally required (e.g., law enforcement, legal compliance)
  • With your explicit consent
---

5. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your information. While no system is 100% secure, we follow best practices, including encryption of data in transit and at rest, secure authentication, and access logging.

---

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. You may request deletion of your account or associated data by contacting us.

---

7. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under that age, we will delete it promptly.

---

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing or request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us through the form provided on our website.

---

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any material changes via the Platform or email. Continued use of the Platform after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.

---

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the contact form on our website or refer to the help center.
