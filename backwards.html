backwards

Recently, I read a post that talked about the benefits of approaching things backwards and I thought I would try it out. The results of the exercise are below.

Overarching goal: to create the next trillion dollar company
Well how do I do this? I make something that many people want, something that is so valuable that it changes lives
How do I do this? I find out what people want
How do I do this? I talk to as many people as possible and consume as much as possible
How do I do this? I go out there and just do it and surround myself with the smartest people in the world

Overarching goal: find and gain the company of the smartest people in the world
How do I do this? Use X, other social media, go to where they all are: San Francisco, currently
How do I do that? Already using X, for SF get funding to get there in some way, to live there
How do I do that? Cold email founders, provide a value proposition that they cannot miss
How do I do that? Find places where these people are concentrated (the most ambitious startups) and reach out to them, showing them why I can aid in the mission to make a trillion dollar company

Overarching goal: to talk to as many people as possible
Well how? Go and talk to people, in the library, at the gym, etc.
How? I am surrounded by people everywhere, may as well speak to them

Overarching goal: to get a gf
How do I do that? Go and talk to as many girls as possible
How do I do that? Be around as many women as possible to make it as easy as it can be
How do I do that? Go to where women go, think like a woman
How do I do that? Go to coffee shops, go to other places besides the library
How else do I do it? Go and just talk to them

Overarching goal: to be the most knowledgeable person on all topics
How do I do that? Read as much as possible and talk to ChatGPT for 15 minutes a day about something random, non‑negotiable
How do I do that? Find the best books to read and read them, look for things recommended by intelligent people – this will create a nice network
How do I do the other? Make 15 minutes, whenever I am doing something and just talk to chat about it

Overarching goal: make a million dollars ARR by the end of the year
How do I do that? Create something people want, something that I want and I would use
How do I get it out there? Use viral marketing, put fliers up, try various marketing strategies that could possibly work How do I do that? Consume content, produce content, learn my way around the algo and how things work
How do I also do that? Create as many things as possible, something is bound to work and my opportunity cost for anything is 0
